The Times Of Indiatoi tech deskApr 28, 2026, 4:33 PM

OpenAI is reportedly testing a new feature called "Chronicle" for its AI coding agent, Codex. However, it has raised privacy and cybersecurity concerns due to its similarity to Microsoft's Windows Recall.
